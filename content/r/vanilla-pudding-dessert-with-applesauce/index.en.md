1. Crumble a few leftover christmas cookies into some glasses
2. Cook the vanilla pudding according to the instructions on the packaging with some milk
3. Now, pour the pudding into the glasses evenly
4. Add two tablespoons of applesauce on top of the pudding
5. Refrigerate the glasses for about an hour, until the pudding has gotten firm
6. Enjoy!
