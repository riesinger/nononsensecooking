1. Rinse the lentils and put them aside for now
2. Dice the onion and sauté it for 1-2 minutes in a pot with a little bit of oil
3. Add the Garam Masala and roast it for another 2-3 minutes
4. Next, add the lentils, diced tomatos and coconut milk and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes, util the lentils are soft enough. Stirr regularly to prevent the lentils from sticking to the pot
5. Season with salt, pepper and chili flakes
6. Enjoy!
